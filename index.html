How to Perform Salah

Salah is the obligatory prayer performed by Muslims five times a day. It involves specific movements and supplications.

  • Fajr: 2 Rakats
  • Dhuhr: 4 Rakats
  • Asr: 4 Rakats
  • Maghrib: 3 Rakats
  • Isha: 4 Rakats

Guide to Fasting (Sawm)

Fasting is an important part of Islam, especially during Ramadan. Fasting involves refraining from food, drink, and other physical needs from dawn to sunset.

Charity in Islam (Zakat)

Zakat is a form of almsgiving and one of the Five Pillars of Islam. It is a religious obligation for Muslims to give a portion of their wealth to those in need.

Performing Hajj and Umrah

Hajj is the pilgrimage to Mecca and is obligatory for every Muslim who is able to do so. Umrah is a lesser pilgrimage that can be performed at any time of the year.

Daily Duas

Here are some important duas for daily life:

  • Before Eating: "Bismillah" (In the name of Allah)
  • Before Sleeping: "Allahumma bismika amutu wa ahya" (O Allah, in Your name I die and live)
  • After Waking Up: "Alhamdulillahilladhi ahyana ba'da ma amatana wa ilayhin-nushoor" (Praise be to Allah who gave us life after taking it from us, and unto Him is the resurrection)
